Vision

Single platform for all Indian Physics enthusiasts with pan India collaborative physics activities and Indian representation in International platforms.

Goal

We are working hand-on-hand with International Association of Physics Students (IAPS) to form the Physics student communities in India with the goal of establishing Association of Indian Physics Students (AIPS) which will represent India in both national and international platforms. One of our core goals is also to make people aware implicitly or explicitly about science, especially Physics and work towards developing a scientific Indian society science driven Indian society.

Mission

  • A university based network of Physics students in India.
  • Physics as a core value, develop a Research and Development (R&D) driven scientific society of India with a global presence.
  • A Global representation team of Indian Physics students.

Invitation

Hereby, we would like to invite you to join us in this mission to increase our footprint across India and collaborate with us in building the network of Indian Physics students. We encourage you to join our mission and make it successful through your valuable contribution and give your community a national presence and an international platform as well as an exposure through to grow together through this channel.
