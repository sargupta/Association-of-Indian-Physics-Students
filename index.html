Our Mission

We are working hand-on-hand with International Association of Physics Students (IAPS) to form the Physics student communities in India with the goal of establishing Association of Indian Physics Students (AIPS) which will represent India in both national and international platforms. One of our core goals is also to make people aware implicitly or explicitly about science, especially Physics and work towards developing a scientific Indian society.
